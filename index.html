Welcome to Srijansheel Home

What We Offer: Detoxification of chemicals, healthy environment for development of body.

Srijansheel Home is a safe and peaceful place for those seeking freedom from addiction. We provide a supportive community where healing and growth begin.

Our mission is to guide every individual through their recovery journey with compassion, care, and proven methods. Whether you're taking your first step or supporting someone you love, we are here for you.

Srijansheel Jeevan Sudhar Kendra is a dedicated rehabilitation center focused on the holistic recovery and personal growth of boys. Our mission is to provide a safe, nurturing environment where young individuals can heal, build life skills, and regain their confidence. We offer personalized programs designed to address mental, emotional and behavioral challenges, helping each boy develop into a responsible and productive member of society. With a compassionate team of trained professionals, we are committed to guiding each young person through their recovery journey and supporting them every step of the way.

With a team of trained counselors, group therapy sessions, and wellness activities, Srijansheel Home creates an environment where anyone can rebuild their life and find hope again.
